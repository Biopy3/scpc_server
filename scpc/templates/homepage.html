Documentation

In order to avoid possible errors of species delimitation and identification imposed by the artificial classification of morphology, we rejected the species status of all our samples and treated them all as completely identical individuals. The computing process was named as Sister Clade Progressive Calculation (SCPC), which implemented as follows: The genetic distances between the sister individuals or assemble were calculated in turn, from the top of the trees to the root. We hypothesize that genetic variation within a species is much smaller than that between species and thus a genetic gap exists. The pairwise distances were counted and their frequency distribution histograms were created. The first gap is the regarded as molecular threshold between species.
